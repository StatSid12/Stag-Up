Elevator Pitch Contest

November 14, 10:00am

DSB Dining Room

Make a pitch. Win a prize. Recruit a mentor.

Learn how to ask for what you really want. This workshop covers the basics of making a business pitch to potential buyers, partners, investors, etc. After a morning session with hands-on training in the essential parts of a business pitch, in the afternoon you will craft a brief recruiting pitch to attract prospective mentors to work with you on your project. The mentors' favorite pitches will be awarded cash prizes at the Showcase in April.
